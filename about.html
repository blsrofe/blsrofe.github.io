When not trying to learn as much as I can as fast as I can, I enjoy baseball, college football, judo, board games, traveling, and spending time enjoying Colorado with my husband and two dogs Dallas and Karma.

Education and Work Experience

I started my education with a B.A. in psychology from the University of Michigan where I attended school on a ROTC scholarship. Upon graduation I served four years in the US Army as a Military Police Officer in various posts and deployments around the world. After leaving the Army I went through an alternative licensure program through Mesa State College to earn my elementary teaching license. I then spent ten years teaching elementary school in an urban district outside of Denver while earning my Masters in Reading Curriculum Development and Instruction.
